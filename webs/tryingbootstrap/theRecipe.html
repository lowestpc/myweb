    Before we begin..

    You need something that can contain the soup, a cooking pot. Basin of Vows will do fine for cooking a soup.

    You can find Basin of Vows in the Tools Page.

    Stirring Tool

    Then you need a tool to stir the soup. You may use any weapon you preferred, In this case we're using the Club since there is no ladle item in this game.Dark souls 2 Best game?

    You can find the Club in the Tools Page.

    Chopping Tool

    We also need a tool to chop the ingredients you can use any sharp weapon you have, but i recommend using the Bandit's Knife

    You can find the Bandit's Knife in the Tools Page.

    Something that Burns

    Then lastly we need something to heat the pot. You can use any Coal or Young White Branch. In this case we're using the Farron's Coal.

    You can find the Farron's Coal in the Tools Page.

    Estus Flask

    You need at least three Chugs of Estus Flask

    Step One

    Take one bundle of Mossfruit then smash it, then put the smashed Mossfruit inside the Basin of Vows.

    Step Two

    Take one bundle of Green Blossom smash it, then put it in the Basin of Vows

    Step Three

    Pour three Chugs of Estus Flasks and one Chug of Ashen Estus Flask in the pot

    Step Four

    Use the Farron's Coal to heat up the Basin of Vows

    Step Five

    Whilst heating the Basin of Vows, take two Green Blossoms then Slice it.

    Step Six

    Take two remaining Mossfruit, then Slice it.

    Step Seven

    Put the sliced Green Blossom and Mossfruit in the Basin of Vows, then stir the soup.

    Step Eight

    When the water boils pour in Divine Blessing or Hidden Blessing, then stir for a minute

    It's Done!

    Now taste the Dark Soup!
