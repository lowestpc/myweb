    Basin of Vows Location

    There are two ways on getting the Basin of Vows and both ways requires for Emma to die.
    First is killing the three Lords of Cinder then she dies and drops the Basin of Vows.
    Second is straight killing Emma then she drops the Basin of Vows.
    Either way she drops the Basin of Vows so feel free to kill her.
